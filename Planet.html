Dragon Ball is a series created by Akira Toriyama that has already accumulated more than 700 episodes. It is important to keep in mind the chronological order to watch Dragon Ball without missing anything along the way. Since throughout its history many types of series have been developed with a very similar name that can lead to confusion. This project is aimed at all those people who want to start watching Dragon Ball from the beginning to the most current episodes of Super.
